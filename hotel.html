Hotels

WBarcelona

This beachfront gem at the end of Passeig Joan de Borbó, designed by Ricardo Bofill, has rooms with enormous windows affording near-endless views of both the sea and city. The clientele by the hotel's infinity pool, and its surrounding cabanas, is decidedly young and chic.

Grand Hotel Central

Relaxed, discreet and exceedingly comfortable, Grand Hotel Central is a very easy place to turn into a happy home base during a trip to Spain’s buzziest city. It's location on Via Laietana, between the Gothic and Born districts places it directly opposite the cathedral, and its rooftop pool is easily one of the best in town.
